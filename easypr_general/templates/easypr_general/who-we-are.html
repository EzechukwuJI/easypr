Company Overview

EasyPR is an online based media content distribution platform. We basically provide access to hundreds of newspapers and online news publishing platforms across Africa. On easyPR platform, companies and individuals can place contents in the media by submitting media contents such as Press releases, news features, interviews and photo news. These contents are then rewritten by easyPR content writing team who turn the contents into publishable contents and place them in the media as earned media.
